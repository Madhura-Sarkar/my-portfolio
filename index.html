✦ Frequently Asked Questions ✦

Do you have any work experience?
Yes. I have been working for the past one year at Century Media 360 as a Junior Web Designer and Developer. During this time, I have worked on real client projects involving website design, frontend development, and design implementation, gaining hands-on industry experience.
What is your role at Century Media 360?
At Century Media 360, I work as a Junior Web Designer and Developer, where I design and develop frontend interfaces, assist in website structure and responsiveness, and collaborate with the internal team. I also support the social media team by creating and suggesting design assets such as creatives, layouts, and visual ideas to maintain brand consistency.
Why should you hire me?
I bring a strong combination of design sense and technical skills, backed by real-world agency experience. My ability to understand both visual aesthetics and frontend implementation allows me to bridge the gap between design and development. I am detail-oriented, adaptable, and consistently eager to improve and deliver high-quality work.
How do you balance design and development?
My primary strength lies in design, where I focus on usability, visual hierarchy, and aesthetics. At the same time, I actively apply these designs through HTML, CSS, Tailwind, and JavaScript, ensuring practical and responsive implementation. This balance helps me create designs that are both visually appealing and technically feasible.
Are you currently a student?
Yes, I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA). My focus is on graphic design, web design, and frontend development, which I believe will contribute to a strong foundation for my future career.
